Experts Google Experts are a global network of experienced product strategists, designers, developers and marketing professionals actively supporting developers, startups and companies changing the world through web and mobile applications. November, 2015 Mountain View, California, USA How?

Technology

Google Developer Experts (GDEs) are pros in one or more Google Technologies and recognised as thought-leaders in their subject. They are active contributors to the developers ecosystems - writing code samples, speaking frequently at conferences, publishing videos, running tutorials or mentoring others.

Marketing

UX / UI

UX/UI Experts bring professional experience and industry-proven techniques to help developers, startups and companies make their web and mobile apps useful as well as beautiful. They define the right user groups, run research, create prototypes and wireframes. Hence turning customer insights into actionable designs.

Product Strategy

Product Strategy Experts have significant strategy experience in startups and companies. They apply their knowledge and experience of multiple business models to sketch out the basis of a product and apply reasoning. They appreciate that developing apps can be high-risk and high-speed business, and use their skills to iterate quickly and guide people through business decisions.
